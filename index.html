What Is Medical Acupuncture?

Insertion of fine needles into specific points, often with manual stimulation or low-frequency currents. Standard course: 5–8 sessions.

Benefits & Science

Needle stimulation releases endorphins and serotonin, modulating pain. Research shows relief for back, knee, neck pain, migraines, tension headaches, and nausea.

Why Choose a Medical Acupuncturist?

We combine medical training with acupuncture expertise, coordinating your care, understanding your history, and ensuring safe, integrated treatment.

Tradition Meets Modern Science

With over 4,000 years of history, acupuncture is now backed by modern research and used in UK pain clinics for evidence-based care.
